Hyderabad Restaurant Chain

×

The Challenge

A popular restaurant chain with 5 locations across Hyderabad was struggling with low online visibility and minimal website traffic. Despite having excellent food and service, they were not appearing in local search results, and their outdated website was not converting visitors into diners.

Our Solution

  • Comprehensive SEO audit and competitor analysis
  • Complete website redesign with mobile-first approach
  • Local SEO optimization for all 5 locations
  • Google My Business optimization
  • Schema markup implementation for rich snippets
  • Content strategy focused on local cuisine and dining experiences

The Results

215% Increase in organic traffic
185% Increase in online reservations
Top 3 Rankings for key local keywords
42% Decrease in bounce rate
"Bubbles Media transformed our online presence completely. We're now booked solid on weekends and have seen a significant increase in weekday diners as well. Their SEO expertise and beautiful website design have been game-changers for our business." - Arjun Reddy, Managing Director
