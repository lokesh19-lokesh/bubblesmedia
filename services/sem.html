Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our SEM and Google Ads services

How much should I budget for Google Ads?

Your ideal Google Ads budget depends on several factors including your industry, competition, geographic targeting, and business goals. In competitive industries like real estate or legal services, you might need a higher budget to see meaningful results. For most businesses in Hyderabad, we recommend starting with a minimum monthly ad spend of ₹30,000-50,000 in addition to management fees. We'll help you determine the most effective budget based on your specific situation during our initial consultation.

How long does it take to see results from Google Ads?

One of the biggest advantages of Google Ads is the speed at which you can start seeing results. Your ads will begin displaying as soon as your campaigns are approved, which typically happens within 24 hours of launch. You'll start getting impressions and clicks immediately, though conversion optimization is an ongoing process. Most campaigns require 2-4 weeks of data collection and optimization to reach peak performance. We provide weekly updates during this initial period and make continuous adjustments to improve results.

What's the difference between SEO and SEM/Google Ads?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving your website's organic (non-paid) visibility in search engine results through technical optimizations, content creation, and link building. It typically takes 3-6 months to see significant results, but provides long-term benefits with no direct cost per click.

SEM (Search Engine Marketing) and Google Ads involve paying for placement in search results or on websites across the internet. You pay each time someone clicks on your ad (PPC - Pay Per Click). SEM delivers immediate visibility and traffic but requires ongoing investment to maintain results.

For most businesses, a combined approach using both SEO and SEM provides the best results, with SEM driving immediate traffic while SEO builds long-term organic presence.

Do you require long-term contracts for Google Ads management?

We offer flexible engagement options to suit your needs. While we recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to allow sufficient time for campaign optimization and to see meaningful results, we don't lock clients into long-term contracts. Our standard agreement includes a 30-day notice period for cancellation. Many of our clients have been with us for years because of the consistent results we deliver, not because they're bound by contract terms.

How do you measure the success of Google Ads campaigns?

We measure success based on your specific business objectives, which might include:

  • Conversion Rate: The percentage of visitors who complete desired actions
  • Cost Per Conversion: How much you pay for each lead or sale
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue generated relative to ad spend
  • Quality Score: Google's rating of your keywords and ads
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): Percentage of people who click your ads
  • Impression Share: How often your ads appear compared to total available impressions

We provide transparent reporting on these metrics and others relevant to your goals, with context and insights to help you understand what the numbers mean for your business.

Can you work with my existing Google Ads account?

Yes, we can take over management of your existing Google Ads account. In fact, this is often preferable as it preserves your account history and Quality Scores. We'll begin with a comprehensive audit of your current campaigns to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement. Based on this analysis, we'll either optimize your existing campaign structure or recommend rebuilding campaigns that aren't performing well. We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your ongoing advertising efforts.
