Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our content marketing services

How long does it take to see results from content marketing?

Content marketing is a long-term strategy that builds momentum over time. While some results can be seen relatively quickly (such as engagement metrics), the full benefits typically develop in phases:

  • 1-3 months: Initial content foundation established, baseline metrics gathered, early engagement indicators
  • 3-6 months: Improved search visibility for targeted keywords, growing traffic, initial lead generation
  • 6-12 months: Significant organic traffic growth, established authority in your niche, consistent lead generation
  • 12+ months: Compounding returns on content investment, strong organic presence, content-attributed revenue

The timeline can vary based on factors such as your industry competitiveness, content quality and frequency, existing online presence, and distribution strategy. We set realistic expectations and provide regular progress updates throughout our partnership.

How do you measure content marketing success?

We measure content marketing success based on your specific business objectives, which might include:

  • Brand Awareness: Website traffic, page views, time on page, social shares
  • Engagement: Comments, social interactions, email open rates, click-through rates
  • Lead Generation: Form submissions, content downloads, email sign-ups
  • SEO Performance: Keyword rankings, organic traffic, backlinks
  • Conversions: Content-attributed sales, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost
  • Customer Retention: Repeat visits, subscriber retention, customer lifetime value

We provide comprehensive reporting that tracks these metrics and explains what they mean for your business, along with strategic recommendations for ongoing improvement.

How do you ensure content is aligned with our brand voice?

Maintaining a consistent brand voice across all content is crucial for building brand recognition and trust. Our process for ensuring content alignment with your brand voice includes:

  1. Brand Voice Discovery: We begin by conducting a thorough analysis of your existing content, brand guidelines, and company values to understand your current voice.
  2. Voice Guidelines Development: Based on our findings, we create detailed brand voice guidelines that define tone, style, vocabulary, and other elements that make your brand unique.
  3. Writer Matching: We assign writers to your account who have experience in your industry and can naturally adapt to your brand voice.
  4. Editorial Review: All content goes through a rigorous editorial process to ensure consistency with your brand voice guidelines.
  5. Client Feedback Loop: We incorporate your feedback on initial content pieces to refine our understanding of your preferred voice and style.
  6. Ongoing Refinement: As our partnership evolves, we continuously refine our approach to better capture and express your brand's unique personality.

This systematic approach ensures that all content we create feels authentically like your brand, regardless of format or channel.

Do you handle content distribution and promotion?

Yes, content distribution and promotion are essential components of our content marketing services. Even the best content needs strategic distribution to reach and impact your target audience. Our content distribution approach includes:

  • Owned Media Distribution: Publishing on your website, blog, email newsletters, and social media channels
  • Earned Media Outreach: Pitching content to industry publications, pursuing guest posting opportunities, and building relationships with influencers
  • Paid Content Amplification: Strategic use of paid promotion channels such as social media advertising, content discovery platforms, and native advertising
  • SEO Optimization: Ensuring content is discoverable through search engines with proper keyword targeting and technical optimization
  • Content Repurposing: Extending the reach of your content by adapting it for different formats and channels
  • Community Engagement: Sharing content in relevant online communities and engaging in discussions to drive visibility

Our Growth and Premium packages include comprehensive distribution strategies, while our Starter package includes basic distribution guidance.

How much input is required from our team?

We understand that your time is valuable, and we've designed our content marketing process to minimize the demands on your internal team while still ensuring content accuracy and alignment with your goals. Here's what you can expect:

  • Initial Onboarding (Higher Involvement): The first 4-6 weeks require more input from your team as we conduct discovery sessions, gather information about your business and industry, and align on strategy and goals.
  • Ongoing Content Production (Minimal Involvement): Once we've established a solid foundation, your involvement can be scaled back to:
    • Monthly content calendar review and approval (30 minutes)
    • Subject matter expert interviews as needed (typically 30-60 minutes per month)
    • Content review and feedback (time varies based on content volume)
  • Strategy Reviews (Quarterly): Quarterly strategy review meetings (60-90 minutes) to assess performance and make adjustments.

We can adapt our process based on your team's availability and preferred level of involvement. Some clients prefer a more hands-off approach, while others want to be deeply involved in the content creation process. We're flexible and can accommodate either preference.

Can you work with our existing content team?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house marketing and content teams, and these partnerships often produce the best results. We can support your existing team in several ways:

  • Strategy Development: Providing strategic direction and content planning while your team handles execution
  • Content Production Support: Supplementing your team's capacity during high-demand periods or for specialized content types
  • Editorial Services: Offering editing, proofreading, and optimization for content created by your internal team
  • Training and Workshops: Providing skill development for your internal content creators
  • Distribution and Promotion: Handling the promotion and distribution of content created by your team
  • Performance Analysis: Delivering in-depth analytics and insights on content performance

We'll work with you to define clear roles and responsibilities that complement your existing resources and fill any gaps in expertise or capacity. Our goal is to integrate seamlessly with your team and processes to maximize the effectiveness of your content marketing efforts.
